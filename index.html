Star Wars:

A Mix and Match Game

In this game you need to find pair for each card.

As soon as you match all cards, new level appers, with incrased cards number and time points. You will get extra 20 seconds per each completed level.

Game ends, when you run out of time (lose) or all cards in deck will be matched (win).

If you lose, you have 2 "continue". When you use "continue", you start from last checkpoint, returning to time points and cards position, preserved at the beginning of last level.
